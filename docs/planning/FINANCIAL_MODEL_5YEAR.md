## Model Assumptions

### User Growth
- **Month 1-3:** 20% MoM growth (early adopters)
- **Month 4-12:** 15% MoM growth (steady growth)
- **Year 2-3:** 10% MoM growth (scaling)
- **Year 4-5:** 7% MoM growth (mature market)

### Conversion Rates
- **Free → Paying:** 15% (industry standard: 10-20%)
- **Annual Churn:** 20% (industry standard: 15-25%)
- **Upgrade Rate:** 20% annually (Basic → Premium → Pro)

### Pricing (Annual, ARR basis)
- Free: $0
- Basic: $100/year
- Premium: $300/year
- Pro: $500/year
- Growth: $5,000/year
- Enterprise: $30,000/year (average)

## Model Notes & Assumptions

**Conservative Assumptions:**
- 15% free-to-paid conversion (industry: 10-20%)
- 20% annual churn (industry: 15-25%)
- No secondary revenue streams (API, transactions)
- Linear user growth (no viral loops)

**Upside Potential Not Modeled:**
- International expansion (3-5x TAM)
- API licensing ($50K-$200K/customer)
- Transaction fees (2-5% of deals)
- White-label partnerships
- Faster enterprise adoption

**Key Variables to Monitor:**
1. Free-to-paid conversion rate
2. Monthly user growth rate
3. Churn rate by tier
4. Upgrade rate (tier movement)
5. CAC by channel
